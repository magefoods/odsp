Important Weekend Release Information

Most banks will release Monday payments on Saturday:

  • ODSP: September 29th (Monday) releases September 27th (Saturday) for many banks
  • Trillium: November 10th (Monday) releases November 8th (Saturday) for many banks
  • CCB: October 20th (Monday) releases October 18th (Saturday) for many banks
